Welcome to Silent Mate Documentation
=====================================

Silent Mate is an intelligent Android app that automatically adjusts your phone's audio profile based on how you're holding or placing your device.

Quick Start
-----------

1. **Install the App**
   
   Download and install Silent Mate on your Android device (5.0+)

2. **Grant Permissions**
   
   * Do Not Disturb Access
   * Notification Permission

3. **Enable Detection**
   
   Toggle the switches for the modes you want to use

4. **Done!**
   
   Silent Mate will now automatically manage your audio profiles
